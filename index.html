Why the Turkish Scholarship?

The Turkish Scholarship, or Turkey in general, will be the perfect place for me to begin my journey. The scholarship will provide me with many essential things that will allow me to focus solely on my education, such as accommodation, a monthly stipend, and more. Additionally, Turkey is one of the leading countries in education, and on top of that, it is a predominantly Muslim country. With mosques everywhere, I will never be far from hearing the call to prayer, which I have grown accustomed to in my home country.

How Will Turkey Benefit Me?

Aside from the high-quality education I will receive, Turkey will play a key role in my story. For instance, the "Ajans Press" agency reported that the car ownership rate is one car for every 7 people, which is quite a good ratio. This presents a great opportunity for me. If I have the chance to travel to Turkey, I aspire to build strong relationships with people there, which will provide me with a chance to establish a business path in Turkey, should I be able to set up my own company in the future.
